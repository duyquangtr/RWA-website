Analysis of Stablecoin Liquidity and Capital Allocation in RWA Protocols

How is RWA tokenization reshaping on-chain liquidity and accelerating the integration of traditional finance with blockchain ecosystems?

In this project, we explore how economic conditions drive capital allocation in DeFi's fastest-growing sector: real-world asset (RWA) protocols. RWA platforms bring traditional assets like Treasuries and private credit onto the blockchain. The result: explosive growth and billions in new capital opportunities backed by stablecoin liquidity.
