Tasks

This project investigates how macro liquidity conditions drive capital allocation in DeFi's RWA sector through six key tasks:

  • Liquidity conditions: M2-based liquidity gauge
  • Stablecoin impact: Market cap vs. protocol growth
  • Interest rate effects: Treasury vs. Private Credit allocation
  • Chain adoption: Multi-chain deployment patterns
  • Market structure: Protocol concentration metrics
  • Preference shifts: Asset type correlations over time

Data Overview

Our analysis combines data from DeFiLlama and FRED APIs to analyze the RWA ecosystem and its relationship to macroeconomic conditions. DeFiLlama tracks decentralized finance protocols, while FRED provides Federal Reserve economic data.

Scale

  • 59,761 daily observations
  • 5 years of data
  • 8 quantitative & 4 categorical variables
  • 12 total features

Coverage

  • 9 RWA protocols
  • 21 blockchain networks
